## 14:48 Europe/Madrid

### Current Objective

- Capture the current experimental `heading_locked` work and pivot planning toward a 3D prototype that preserves the same low camera angle and control feel without continuing to grow the custom 2D pseudo-perspective renderer.

### Decisions Taken

- The current 2D pseudo-3D experiment proved the gameplay direction:
- `heading_locked` control scheme is valid
- player anchored low on screen is desirable
- front tactical vision plus broader rear readability is desirable
- However, the custom 2D projection has become too expensive relative to the value it provides.
- The next recommended implementation direction is a 3D prototype, not more investment in the fake 2.5D renderer.
- The 3D prototype should preserve:
- the current camera angle/look from the experimental `heading_locked` view
- the hybrid FPS / third-person-shooter feel
- 180 degree frontal tactical vision with occlusion
- 180 degree rear readability with no hiding
- HUD/minimap/settings flow where practical
- Player and enemy visuals in the 3D prototype should start as simple capsules.
- Player collision and hitbox should also start as simple capsules.
- Exterior walls that sit between the camera and the interior should become see-through / ghosted rather than fully blocking the view when viewed from behind.
